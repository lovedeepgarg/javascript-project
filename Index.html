About "Tic-Tac-Toe" Game

Tic Tac Toe, also known as noughts and crosses, is a classic game loved by people of all ages. Its origins can be traced back to ancient Egypt, where it was played on temple walls. Over time, it has evolved into the popular paper-and-pencil game we know today.

This digital version of Tic Tac Toe brings the timeless fun of the game to your device, allowing you to play anytime, anywhere.

How to Play

Tic Tac Toe is played on a 3x3 grid. Two players take turns marking empty cells with their chosen symbol, typically 'X' and 'O'. The goal is to get three of your symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, before your opponent does.

To win, you need to think strategically and anticipate your opponent's moves. The game ends when one player achieves three symbols in a row, or if the grid is filled without a winner (resulting in a draw).
